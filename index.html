  • Feb 2015

    Digital Ramp Counter

    I was tasked with the design and implementation of a digital circuit which would display a binary represenation of an varying alalog voltage. The device would "count up" to the voltage display using a ADC, a series of flip-flops, a clock and a latch. The circuit was designed and simulated in National Instrument's MultiSIM before being successfully constructed in the lab.
